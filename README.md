## 🛠️ Using Your Assistant

When you open the application, a simple window appears. You see a text box at the bottom. This is where you talk to your AI assistant. You can type things like "summarize this file" or "draft an email for me." 

The application uses skills to help you. A skill is a pre-set action the software can perform. These include tasks like searching your local folders, reading text files, or formatting data into a list. You do not need to activate these skills manually. The assistant detects what you need and picks the right tool for the job.

If the assistant asks for permission, read the prompt closely. It might ask to read a file or open a website. Clicking "Allow" lets the assistant finish the task. You keep control of the software at all times.

## ⚙️ Settings and Customization

You can change how the assistant behaves through the Settings menu. Open the top menu by clicking the gear icon.

*   Display Mode: Switch between light and dark themes based on your room lighting.
*   Assistant Voice: Choose a tone for your AI helper. Options include formal, casual, and concise.
*   Data Privacy: Choose which folders the assistant can access. You can restrict the assistant to read-only access for sensitive documents.
*   Reset: Clear your chat history if you want a fresh start.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Safety

Privacy remains a priority. The application stores your history locally on your machine. We do not track your conversation logs or sell your data. When the assistant sends a request to the AI model, it sends only the specific text needed for that task. All other files on your computer stay private.

## 📑 Common Questions

What if the application stops responding?

Does the software cost money?
The software is free to download and use. We provide it as an open tool for anyone to enjoy. 

Can I install this on a work computer?
Yes, but check with your employer if they have specific policies regarding AI software. The program operates within standard user folders and does not require deep system administrative rights.

Is my data sent to third parties?
No. Your requests go to the AI provider to generate an answer. They do not use your interactions to train their general models if you use the standard settings. You can review the privacy section in the settings menu to manage these settings.
